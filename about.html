Frequently Asked Questions

What design tools do you use?

I primarily use Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch, and Figma for my design work, as well as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for front-end development.

How do you stay up-to-date with industry trends?

I regularly attend design conferences, take online courses, and follow industry-leading blogs and podcasts to stay informed about the latest trends and best practices in the design world.

What is your approach to user testing?

I believe in conducting user testing throughout the design process to ensure the best possible user experience. I use a combination of usability testing, A/B testing, and user feedback to refine and improve my designs.
